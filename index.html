What gets automated for NTS.

11 workflows running manually today — handled automatically from day one.

01 Sales

Quote Follow-Up Automation

Sales ops only close when won — unchased quotes are lost revenue. Automated follow-up sequences triggered at 3, 7 and 14 days after a quote goes out. No one has to remember.

Trigger: Quote sent, no response received
02 Job Cards

Job Card AI Review & Correction

400–500 completions/month reviewed manually at 5 min average. AI checks spelling, grammar, missing fields and professionalism on every card automatically at completion.

Trigger: Job marked complete in BigChange
03 Job Cards

Custom Field & Format Validation

Wrong character in the pipe sign field breaks the form entirely. AI validates the format on every submitted worksheet and corrects it automatically — before the office ever sees it.

Trigger: Worksheet submitted by engineer
04 CRM

Call Transcription → BigChange Note

Every call transcribed, summarised and pushed into BigChange as a structured note against the right job. Full call history per job automatically — no manual write-up needed.

Trigger: Call ends in Concierge Support
05 Jobs

Group Job Linking by Reference

Related jobs sharing the same client reference automatically linked under the correct parent group in BigChange. Clean visibility across all work under one reference — no manual tidying.

Trigger: Job created with matching reference
06 Sales

PO Received → Attached to Sales Op

Client sends a PO — it's automatically matched to the open sales op and attached in BigChange. The right person is notified to progress. Manual matching eliminated entirely.

Trigger: PO email received from client
07 Jobs

Group Job Template from Quote Duration

Quote specifies 3 days on site — a 3-day group job is built in BigChange automatically. No manual job creation per day. Structure matches exactly what was quoted.

Trigger: Quote accepted with multi-day duration
08 Sales

Quote Accepted → Sales Op Won (One Action)

Two separate manual clicks consolidated into one. Accepting a quote automatically marks the sales op as won — eliminating the risk of one being updated without the other.

Trigger: Quote status updated to accepted
09 Engineering

Parts Used → Cross-Check Workflow

Engineer logs parts used on site. AI cross-checks against what was expected or quoted for that job. Discrepancies flagged before the job closes — billing errors caught at completion.

Trigger: Job complete with parts logged
10 CRM

Order Update Emails → BigChange Note

Order update emails automatically matched to the correct job or sales op and attached as a note in BigChange. Full paper trail created without anyone copying anything manually.

Trigger: Order update email received
11 CRM

Job Confirmation Emails → Dialogue

Job confirmation emails from clients logged directly into the BigChange dialogue on the correct job record. Complete communication history per job in one place — automatically.

Trigger: Job confirmation email received
